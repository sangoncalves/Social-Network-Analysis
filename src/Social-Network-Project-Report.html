2.1.1 What does it mean substantively in this case?

If we consider the flow of information, where we want to spread the information than the most efficient person to spread it would be Jean (information reach all network faster) and the last efficient one would be Ellen (takes long for the information to reach all network). In this case, the more central a person (node) is, the closer he/she is from all other people (nodes) in the network.
